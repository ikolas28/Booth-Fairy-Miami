Data Deletion Instructions

How to request deletion of personal information connected to Booth Fairy Miami inquiries or bookings.

How To Request Deletion

To request deletion of personal information connected to a Booth Fairy Miami inquiry, email boothfairyllc@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request.”

Include This Information

Please include your name, email address, phone number if used, Instagram handle if applicable, and a short note saying which inquiry or booking you want deleted. We may need to verify that you are the person connected to the record before deleting it.

What We Delete

We will delete or anonymize personal lead information that is no longer needed for business, legal, accounting, security, or service-delivery reasons. Some records may need to be retained when required for completed transactions, tax records, signed agreements, fraud prevention, dispute handling, or legal compliance.

Instagram Users

If you contacted Booth Fairy Miami through Instagram, include your Instagram handle in the deletion request. We will remove or anonymize CRM lead records and message summaries that are not required for legitimate business or legal purposes.

Response Time

Booth Fairy Miami will review deletion requests as soon as practical and typically responds within 30 days.
