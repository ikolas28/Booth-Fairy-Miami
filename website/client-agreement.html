Agreement Terms

Booth Fairy Miami agrees to provide the selected services for the event listed above. Photo booth services are digital only and include high-quality DSLR digital photos and instant digital sharing when internet or cellular service allows it. Booth Fairy Miami does not currently offer printed photos or 360 photo booth services.

A booking is not confirmed until calendar availability has been checked, this agreement has been signed, and the required 50% non-refundable retainer has been received. The remaining 50% balance is due on the event date before services begin unless otherwise approved in writing.

The client is responsible for accurate event details, venue access, safe setup space, power access, parking/loading instructions, design approvals, music requests, and any venue rules needed for professional service.

The 50% retainer is non-refundable because it reserves the event date and prevents Booth Fairy Miami from accepting another booking for that date/time. Date, venue, package, or service changes must be approved in writing and are subject to availability.
